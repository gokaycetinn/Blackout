Land of pixels - Laboratory pixel art tileset Top down

A downloadable asset pack

Download NowName your own price

First on the Land of pixels  series of top down tilesets. This Laboratory tileset was done because Laboratories are fun! 

This set includes 4 types of floors and doors, 3 types of walls and liquids, 3 fx sprite sheets, as well objects that you may expect to find in a modern laboratory. 

There is an energy orb that has 2 types of fx and a pipe with 1 fire fx effect. Liquids do have a sprite sheet for movement. And the body floating on the green tube has animation as well!

This tileset also includes numbers from 0 to 9, an elevator , some screens, stairs and more!

The ZIP contains some examples of the animations, and how to put the doors on the walls. Also, the tileset comes in 3 sizes, 16px, 32px and 48px! (The expansion file does not include a 32 px nor 48 px sizes)

As for the license, you can do pretty much anything you need to do on this tileset to fit it onto your game/project.

If you want to help me produce more tilesets, or just to show me some support, my ko-fi is always open for that, and I as a good Colombian girl, I do like coffe. 

I hope these pixels in the form of tiles and assets provides you some help for your project!


The new Expansion pack includes new assets, new monsters, new floors and walls, portals and plants!
